About Sunrise Manor

Sunrise Manor was founded with a simple mission: to revolutionize how local businesses generate leads. We recognized that traditional advertising platforms were becoming increasingly expensive and unpredictable, leaving small and medium-sized businesses struggling to compete. Our team of AI specialists and marketing veterans came together to create a solution that would level the playing field. By leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology, specifically Claude AI from Anthropic, we built a system that captures, engages, and converts prospects automatically—without requiring businesses to spend thousands on ad platforms.

Today, we're committed to empowering local businesses to achieve sustainable growth through intelligent automation. Our core values center on transparency, reliability, and measurable results. We believe every business deserves access to enterprise-grade marketing technology, regardless of size or budget. We're not just building software; we're building partnerships with our clients, investing in their success, and proving that AI can be a force for good in the business world. When you work with Sunrise Manor, you're working with a team that genuinely cares about your growth and is dedicated to delivering results that exceed your expectations.
